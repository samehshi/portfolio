## Professional Portfolio Content Standards Research

Based on industry best practices research, professional about pages should:

### Best Practices ✅
1. **Focus on verifiable achievements** - Use specific certifications, education, and measurable outcomes
2. **Emphasize technical competencies** - List specific tools, technologies, and methodologies
3. **Include concrete examples** - Reference specific projects or case studies
4. **Maintain professional tone** - Avoid marketing language and subjective assessments
5. **Quantify experience** - Use specific timeframes and measurable results where possible

### Content Standards
1. **Verifiable Claims Only**: All professional statements should be factual and verifiable
2. **Objective Language**: Replace subjective terms with objective descriptions
3. **Specific Examples**: Use concrete project examples rather than generic capability statements
4. **Professional Tone**: Maintain formal, business-appropriate language throughout
5. **Measurable Outcomes**: When possible, include specific metrics or achievements
