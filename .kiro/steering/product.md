# Product Overview

This is a professional portfolio website for Sameh Shehata Abdelaziz, a BI & Data Analytics Specialist. The site showcases professional achievements, projects, CV, and certifications.

## Key Features
- Academic-style portfolio layout using the al-folio Jekyll theme
- Professional CV with dynamic components and modern design
- Project showcase with detailed case studies and visualizations
- Responsive design with dark/light mode support
- SEO optimized with Google Analytics integration

## Target Audience
- Potential employers and clients in data analytics field
- Professional network and industry peers
- Academic and research communities

## Content Focus
- Data analysis projects and case studies
- Professional experience and achievements
- Technical skills and certifications
- Business intelligence solutions and impact metrics

## Brand Identity
- Professional, clean, and academic aesthetic
- Data-driven storytelling approach
- Emphasis on measurable business impact
- Modern design with accessibility considerations
