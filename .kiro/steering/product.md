# Product Overview

This is a personal portfolio website for Sameh Shehata Abdelaziz, a BI & Data Insights Analyst. The site showcases:

- Professional experience and achievements in data analytics
- Portfolio of data science projects and case studies
- CV/resume with detailed work history and certifications
- Contact information and professional links

The website serves as a professional online presence to demonstrate expertise in data analytics, business intelligence, and machine learning to potential employers, clients, and collaborators.

## Key Features

- Responsive design optimized for all devices
- Project showcase with detailed case studies
- Interactive CV with structured data
- Contact form integration via Formspree
- SEO optimization and analytics tracking
- Dark/light theme support
- Professional academic styling
