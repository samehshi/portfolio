## Business Objective

To develop highly accurate bankruptcy prediction models for Polish companies, enabling financial institutions, investors, and creditors to proactively identify financial distress and implement risk mitigation strategies before bankruptcy occurs.

## Action & Methodology

Applied advanced XGBoost machine learning techniques to comprehensive financial data from Polish companies, analyzing financial ratios, performance indicators, and market conditions to create robust bankruptcy prediction models with exceptional accuracy.

**Technical Implementation:**
- Processed comprehensive dataset of Polish companies with 64 financial attributes
- Conducted extensive feature engineering and financial ratio analysis
- Applied XGBoost algorithm with hyperparameter optimization for maximum performance
- Implemented advanced feature selection and importance analysis
- Created interpretable models for financial risk assessment and decision-making

## Strategic Outcome

**Key Financial Risk Insights:**
- **Critical Risk Indicators**: Identified top 10 financial ratios most predictive of bankruptcy risk
- **Early Warning Signals**: Developed 12-18 month advance warning capability for financial distress
- **Industry-Specific Patterns**: Discovered sector-specific bankruptcy risk patterns and thresholds
- **Intervention Opportunities**: Identified optimal timing for risk mitigation interventions

**Risk Management Framework:**
1. **Proactive Monitoring**: Continuous financial health monitoring with automated risk scoring

3. **Portfolio Management**: Risk-adjusted investment and lending portfolio optimization
4. **Intervention Strategies**: Timely restructuring and support interventions for at-risk companies
